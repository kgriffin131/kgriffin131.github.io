The Timken Company: Process Design and Development Engineering Internship Summer 2020

The Timken Company is located across the globe and manufacures high quality bearings for just about every mechanical market. This internship was located at the World Headquarters in North Canton, Ohio, and I was positioned in the Process Design and Development department. This department deals with the efficiency and technical aspects of manufacuring processes within bearing plants. My specific job entailed the following: Creating and revising engineering drawings using an in-house automation system to be used in plants worldwide, Correcting and adding features to 3D models (Siemens NX) created by the automation system, Worked with data (JMP) to assist in cutting heat treat process timing down by 20%, and Networked with more full-time employees to further my knowledge of the company and industry. This internship was worked during the start of Covid, and consisted of in-person and remote work. At the end of the internship term, I was reviewed by my manager based on goals set at the beginning of the term.
