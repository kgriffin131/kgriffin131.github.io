E-Bike

The objective of the project is to convert a stock mountain bike into an electric bike. I will be achieving this goal by replacing the back wheel with an electric wheel. For power, I will be manufacturing my own battery pack from 20-24 18650 batteries. By wiring them in series and parallel, I can achieve the desired voltage and capacitance for my needs. I am using the design process to professionalize this project. This is a work in progress
